Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026 · Effective from: July 21, 2026

This policy explains which cookie files and local storage technologies we use on mlmpsychology.com and why. In short: we use only what is strictly necessary for the platform to function. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies for marketing purposes.

1.What cookies and local storage are

Cookies are small text files saved by your browser. "Local storage" (localStorage and sessionStorage) is a similar technology, with which the application saves information directly in your browser, without automatically sending it to the server. We use them for the basic operation of the service.

2.What we use and why

Our platform mainly uses functional local storage, not classic tracking cookies:

ItemTypePurposeDuration
Authentication session Local / session storage Keeps you logged in after signing in, so you do not have to re-enter your password on every page For the duration of the session / until logout
Fields filled in the form localStorage Remembers the data you entered (about you and the product) so you do not have to rewrite it each time Until you delete it from your browser
Onboarding status localStorage Remembers that you have completed the usage guide, so it is not shown to you again Until you delete it from your browser
Stripe (at payment) Third-party cookie, essential Fraud prevention and payment security, on the checkout page According to Stripe's policy
We do not use Google Analytics, advertising pixels, or tracking cookies to profile you for marketing purposes.

3.Third-party services

Some components of the site are provided by third parties and may process minimal technical data (for example, the IP address) when their content is loaded:

  • Stripe — secure payment processing (may set essential cookies for fraud prevention).
  • Supabase — authentication (manages your session token, stored locally in the browser).
  • Google Fonts — display of fonts; when loaded, the IP address may be visible to the provider.

Details on how we process data are in the Privacy Policy.

4.Consent

The items we use are strictly necessary for the operation of the platform and the security of payments. Under the law, strictly necessary technologies do not require prior consent. Because we do not use marketing or tracking cookies, we do not show you a consent banner for such purposes. If in the future we introduce non-essential technologies, we will ask for your consent in advance.

5.How to control or delete them

You can delete the data saved by the site at any time from your browser settings (history / browsing data / cookies and site data section). You can also block cookies from your browser settings — but bear in mind that blocking essential items may prevent login or payment from working properly.

6.Changes

We may update this policy. The date of the last update is shown at the top. By continuing to use the site after an update, you acknowledge the new version.
