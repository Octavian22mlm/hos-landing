2.What data we collect

a) Account data

Your email address and password (stored in encrypted form, not in plain text). We do not have access to your password.

b) Payment data

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your card details. We keep only the technical identifiers necessary to manage the subscription (for example, the Stripe customer and subscription identifiers) and possibly the billing data managed by Stripe.

c) Service usage data

Your plan, the number of remaining scripts, the date of the last allocation, a log of generations (when and how they were generated), and the scripts generated for you.

d) Content entered into the generator

The information you fill in the form: about you (gender, approximate age, approach style, experience), about the product, and about the person you intend to contact (see section 4).

e) Support messages

If you write to us through the help form, we keep the message, your email address, and your plan, so that we can reply to you.

f) Technical data

As with most websites, our servers and providers may process technical data such as IP address, browser and device type, necessary for operation and security (including the loading of web fonts).

4.Data of the invited person

By design, we collect as little data as possible about the person you intend to contact. We do not ask for or collect the surname, phone number, email address, postal address, or other contact or direct identification data of that person.

The available fields are limited to general context elements (for example, an optional name, an approximate age, the type of relationship, a general professional or family context) — used solely to generate your script. We do not attempt, nor are we able, to identify that person on the basis of this information.

Your responsibility as a user. You are the one who enters this information, based on your direct relationship with the person. You agree:

  • not to enter data that directly identifies the person (full name, contact details, address, etc.);
  • not to enter into the free-text fields sensitive data — for example relating to health, intimate private life, beliefs, or orientation — which are not necessary and should not be provided;
  • to use the generated scripts in a lawful and ethical manner in your communication.

Further details on your responsibility are in the Terms and Conditions.

10.Cookies and local storage

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies for marketing purposes. This data stays in your browser and you can delete it at any time from your browser settings. Details in the Cookie Policy.
